The Effect of Geographical Locations on College Tuition Across the United States

In this paper, we studied different causes for the fluctuations in this difference in cost in four major regions of the US, the Northeast, Midwest, West, and South. We found that higher education costs different amounts of money in different areas due to the lack of national regulation of higher education funding, which causes variance in the average cost of college tuition in different regions.
